About Us

This is Juan R. Liwag Memorial High School

Juan R. Liwag Memorial High School is known for the motto "Per aspera ad astra" which translates "to the stars through difficulties," this quote depicts the realization that one has to go through the bridge paving their way into their dream destination, however the latter is expected to be filled with difficulties and hardships that we are tasked to surpass in order to reach our desire to succeed, it is also a form of learning for us since challenges are made for us to learn and to experience, so that we can expand our capabilities, and turn our weaknesses into strengths to easily triumph against the hinderances. In the end, as the doors of opportunities unveil, a new chapter has come, and we can finally say "VICTORY!"

Juan R. Liwag Memorial National High School has changed their name from Gapan Municipal High School since 1946, up to its present name that we are all familiar with. Furthermore, JRLMHS has made itself a household name as one of the biggest public secondary school in Gapan City, people were intrigued as they want an education that will secure their future, and will also be a bridge for them in order to reach what they envisioned to be years ahead.

Programs

STE

Science, Technology, and Engineering Program (STE) provides skills in the field of science and technology when undertaking more advanced science subjects.

SPA

Special Program in the Arts (SPA), this program offers a comprehensive arts-based education to students with an inclination to the arts. It also helps students to improve their creativity.

SPJ

Special Program in Journalism (SPJ) helps to enrich the experiences, hone the journalistic skills and competencies of student and to strengthen free and responsible journalism

SPED

Special Education Program (SPED)/ Inclusive Education, this program serves to help students with disabilities by doing a more inclusive approach to education.

SPS

Special Program in Sports (SPS) is a special program which shall lay the foundation for a career and/or support to a career for learners with interest, skills and aptitude in Sports.

SPFL

Special Program in Foreign Language (SPFL) is a program that helps learners develop skills in listening, reading, writing, speaking, and viewing second foreign languages.
